Salesforce Data Migration Global Implementation

Project Overview

Standardized and modernized CRM systems across multiple international business units (Americas, EMEA, APAC) into a unified Salesforce platform, improving global customer engagement, data quality, reporting capabilities, and process efficiency.

My Role

Senior Business Analyst leading a 12-member Agile cross-functional team including PM, Developers, Salesforce Architects, QA, and Business SMEs.

  • Conducted stakeholder interviews across three regions to gather pain points and understand regional custom processes
  • Facilitated global workshop to align on CRM vision and define high-level business objectives

Outcomes

  • Reduced CRM-related support tickets
  • Improved sales pipeline visibility globally with real-time reporting
  • Increased adoption rate within 60 days post-launch
